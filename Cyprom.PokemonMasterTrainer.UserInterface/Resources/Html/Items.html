Item Cards

During your adventure, you will find certain items. This can happen when you win a Pokémon battle, when you arrive in a city, or even as an event. You can benefit from these items in different ways. The list below sums up the possibilities:

Attack Bonus
Add bonus damage to your Pokémon's attack during a battle.
Poké Ball
Special Poké Balls increase your odds when trying to catch a Pokémon.
  • Great Ball (shown): You may add 1 to your roll (optional).
  • Ultra Ball: You may add or subtract 1 to/from your roll (optional).
  • Master Ball: Catch without fail, though you still have to roll (there is only 1 Master Ball in the game).
Fly
Fly to any city or town (instead of rolling a die).
Potion
A potion revives one of your knocked out Pokémon.
Poké Doll
If another trainer forces you to battle or to trade, you can use this card to stop him.

Bag

Every trainer has a bag which can hold a maximum of 7 items. If you have more than 7 item cards at the end of your turn, you must discard some of them. After using or discarding an item card, it is added to the bottom of the deck. Only the Master Ball is left out until a new game is started.
