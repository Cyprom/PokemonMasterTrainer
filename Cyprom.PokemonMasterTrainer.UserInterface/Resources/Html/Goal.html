Goal

Power Points

The goal of the game is to be the first of all players to become a Pokémon Master. The first step in achieving this goal, is reaching Indigo Plateau. A ferry from Cinnabar Island can take you there, provided that you have accumulated at least 20 power points. Power points are a Pokémon attribute. The big yellow number on the right side of a Pokémon chip shows the amount of power points the Pokémon has. (Example: Gyarados has 7 Power Points)
Your total amount of power points is the sum of all power points of your Pokémon that are fit for battle. This means that Pokémon that were knocked out in battle and haven't been revived yet, do not add their power points to your total.

Beat the Elite

When you have reached Indigo Plateau, you have to aim for a "BATTLE" space. Landing on this space will give you the opportunity to fight against an elite trainer. After beating this trainer, you have proven yourself a true Pokémon Master and you have won the game. For details on the elite battle, go to the Indigo Plateau instruction page.
