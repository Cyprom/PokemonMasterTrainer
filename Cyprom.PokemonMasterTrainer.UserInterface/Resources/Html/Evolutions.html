Evolutions

Evolving

Some Pokémon evolve into stronger forms. As this game is set up however, you will never actually evolve any of your Pokémon. Here, you can only get an evolution bonus if you obtain Pokémon in the same line of evolution. This bonus comes in the form of attack damage for the higher level Pokémon.

Set of Two

If you collect both Pokémon in an evolution line of 2, the evolution bonus is +3 for the second Pokémon. For example: if you have both Pikachu and Raichu, Raichu's attack damage is boosted to 9!

Set of Three

If you collect all three Pokémon in an evolution line of 3, the evolution bonus is +5 for the highest Pokémon. In case you only have 2 out of 3 or you use the second in line, then a bonus of +2 is given. For example: If you have Pidgey and Pidgeotto, Pidgeotto's attack damage is boosted to 6. If you have Pidgeotto and Pidgeot, Pidgeot's attack damage is boosted to 8. If you get hold of all three, Pidgeotto's attack is boosted to 6, but Pidgeot gets a total damage of 11!
